Product Positioning

MatchBuzz is an AI x global expansion product for football campaign execution.

MatchBuzz is not positioned as a generic sports news page. It is built as a workflow product for overseas football communities, sports creators, and brand operators that need content, interaction, and reusable assets around major tournament moments.

The current MVP covers one full loop: match selection, localized generation, live poll creation, support engagement, and share-card export.

The business model is also clearer now: capture football attention like Hupu through ads and sponsorship, then convert high-intent traffic like Taopiaopiao through ticketing, event packages, and commerce flows.
