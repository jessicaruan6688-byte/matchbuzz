Autonomous World Cup campaign workflows for overseas football audiences.

MatchBuzz is an AI product for global football content operations. It targets overseas fan communities, sports creators, and brand social teams in markets such as Indonesia, Latin America, and English-speaking creator ecosystems. Instead of simple Q&A, it runs a usable workflow: pick a match, localize the angle, generate content, launch interaction, and export campaign assets.
